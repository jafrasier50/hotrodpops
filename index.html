Made in
Texas

Hot Rod Pops started in Houston in 2015, where lowriders, murals, and popsicle carts share the same blocks. We took the ice pops we grew up on and gave them a paint job.

Every pop is still made by hand: fresh fruit, authentic Mexican cane sugar, small batches. That's the whole recipe. Fruit, sugar, and pride.

Texas made. Texas proud.
